Animal Info: Penguine

Clasification

Class - Aves This class includes all birds. Birds have an outer covering of feathers, are endothermic (warm-blooded), have front limbs modified into wings, and lay eggs.

Order - Sphenisciformes This order includes all living and extinct penguins. Family - Spheniscidae Spheniscidae includes all penguins, living and extinct, and is the only family classification in the order Sphenisciformes.
